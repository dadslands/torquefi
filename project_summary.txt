The rusEFI project is dedicated to developing a GPL-licensed open-source Engine Control Unit (ECU). It thrives as a community-driven initiative, aiming to provide a highly flexible and customizable engine management solution for automotive enthusiasts and developers. By leveraging STM32 microcontrollers for its core hardware and offering a suite of software tools including firmware, tuning software, and a simulator, rusEFI empowers users to tailor engine performance to specific needs.
